Content Detection

Please read our Terms and Conditions and Community Guidelines for content detection and moderation.

Community Guidelines

Only the following will be automatically blocked (videos and shorts):

  • Sexual content – video or audio with sexual or adult material.
  • Real violence – video or audio with real violence or graphic harm.

Creator videos are not blocked. When you upload content that uses another creator's work (e.g. songs, funny clips, or other videos), the video will be shown and the original creator's channel logo will be displayed on it, so viewers see who the original creator is. We do not block such content – we show it with attribution.

Same applies to videos and shorts.
